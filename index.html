Frequently Asked Questions

Are the QR codes generated here free to use? ▼
Yes, all QR codes generated on this site are 100% free for personal and commercial use. No watermarks, no sign-up required.
Do the QR codes expire? ▼
No. QR codes generated here are static codes that never expire. They encode data directly into the code pattern, so they work forever.
What types of QR codes can I create? ▼
You can create QR codes for URLs/links, plain text, WiFi network credentials, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, and vCards (digital business cards).
Can I customize the colors of my QR code? ▼
Yes, you can customize both the foreground (dark) and background (light) colors of your QR code. Make sure there is enough contrast for scanners to read it reliably.
What file formats can I download? ▼
You can download your QR code as a PNG image (best for web and print) or as an SVG vector file (best for high-resolution printing and scaling to any size).
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. All QR code generation happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to a server. Your WiFi passwords, contact details, and other information remain completely private.
What is error correction and which level should I use? ▼
Error correction allows QR codes to be read even if partially damaged or obscured. Low (7%) creates smaller codes. Medium (15%) is a good default. High (30%) is best if the code might be printed small or get worn. Higher correction = larger QR code.
